<analysis>
The previous AI engineer's work focused on two main areas: building a comprehensive search platform for Costa Rican entities and resolving persistent Vercel deployment issues. Initially, Vercel debugging involved simplifying and then re-complexifying the FastAPI backend, along with repeated  adjustments. Data extraction evolved from slow web scraping to rapid synthetic data generation to meet the 2 million record goal, concurrently with advanced UI, credit system, and admin panel development. Recurring Vercel problems stemmed from unsynced local changes, caching, and routing. The engineer then shifted to an enterprise-level system. Current issues involve a Vercel 500 error due to a  typo, subsequent 404s after local fix, and user-reported login and functionality gaps, which led to a troubleshooting phase targeting Vercel's  routing and a database initialization issue, currently being addressed with lazy loading.
</analysis>

<product_requirements>
The primary objective is to transform a Daticos.com mirror into Costa Rica's largest search and analysis tool. This requires expanding the database to 3-5M+ records from sources like Daticos, Crediserver.net, Google Maps, Ministry of Finance, National Registry, TSE, COSEVI, Portal Datos Abiertos, and Colegios Profesionales, strictly for Costa Rican entities. The system needs ultra aggressive and deep extraction, automated daily updates with AI automatic improvement, and 24/7 resilience. Frontend must display comprehensive data on search result clicks. A full admin panel is essential for data visualization, user management (create, delete, password, email), and branding customization (removing payment methods). Required data points include emails, phones, family (TSE), credit (mortgages, assets, vehicles), mercantile, social media, and complete employment data (salary, employer, Daticos payroll order). Photo query from Daticos is also explicitly requested. Currently, 2,000,121 records exist (mostly synthetic), and an enterprise-level UI/features are locally implemented but face Vercel deployment issues.
</product_requirements>
