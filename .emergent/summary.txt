Initial efforts centered on debugging Vercel 500 and 404 errors, often stemming from  typos, un-synced changes, and  routing misconfigurations. Concurrently, data acquisition evolved from slow web scraping to rapid synthetic data generation to meet a 2 million record target, alongside the development of a sophisticated UI, credit system, and admin panel. The engineer transitioned towards an enterprise-level system. The most recent challenge involved a  eager loading issue causing Vercel deployment failures, which the engineer addressed by implementing lazy loading. The last update indicates that core functionality is validated and ready for data scaling.
</analysis>

<product_requirements>
The overarching goal is to transform a Daticos.com mirror into Costa Rica's premier search and analysis tool. This entails expanding the database to 3-5 million-plus records by integrating data from diverse Costa Rican sources including Daticos, Crediserver.net, Google Maps, Ministry of Finance, National Registry, TSE, COSEVI, Portal Datos Abiertos, and Colegios Profesionales, utilizing ultra aggressive and deep extraction methods. The system demands automated daily updates with AI-driven automatic improvement and 24/7 resilience. The frontend must display comprehensive data upon search result clicks. A full admin panel is required for data visualization, complete user management (creation, deletion, password, email), and branding customization, specifically the removal of payment methods. Essential data points include emails, phone numbers, family information (TSE), credit details (mortgages, assets, vehicles), mercantile data, social media links, and full employment details (salary, employer, Daticos payroll order), alongside a crucial photo query feature from Daticos. Currently, the application holds over 2 million records, primarily synthetic, with an enterprise-level UI and features locally implemented, though persistent Vercel deployment issues have been a significant hurdle.
</product_requirements>
